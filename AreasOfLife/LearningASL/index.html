Hi folks!

This is Cyborg Chameleon, attemption to learn ASL. I started learning ASL because I was born with severe hearing loss. I also felt that I have lost the chance of being part of the community that resonates with my identity. I also still have a friend part of ASL speaking community and I wished to be able to talk fluently with them. So feel free to learning to sign with me.

I'm currently learning from Learn How To Sign. Currently trying to finish their playlist

Meredith, the most recomended ASL youtuber as of 2022 01 20, have also suggested the following youtubers to follow to learn from.

  • ASL Meredith
  • ASL Basics (Chris Gorges)
  • Laura Berg Life
  • Signed With Heart
  • ASL That!
